IGR Dashboard

The IGR Dashboard was launched by the Nigeria Governors’ Forum on February 15, 2017,
as one of its flagship programmes to support State governments raise domestic revenues.
The platform is designed to assess the tax/revenue environment of States,track the impact of tax
reforms,and facilitate the sharing of commendable practices through peer learning and technical assistance.

How It Works Data Graphs

How it works

Step one

SIRS vists the IGR Dashboard Via www.ngf.org.ng/igrdashboard

Step Two

State completes monthly data form
and yearly questionnaires.

Step Three

Submissions are Uploaded real time

Step Four

State reports are developed and presented to Governors,the SIRS and JTB.

Theory of change

Advocacy Tools/Instrument

Evidence: Data/Research/Analysis through the IGR Dashboard based on assessment questionnaires submitted by State Boards of Internal Revenue (SBIR)

Decision-Maker Engagement(Monthly engagement with the 36 State Governors at NGF meetings)

Capacity Building for State Tax Officials(via workshops, peer learning events and State visits)

Key Decision-Makers & Influencers

Key Decision Makers 36 State Governors

Influencers / Partnerships:
Joint Tax Board (JTB), 36 State Boards of Internal Revenue & Development Partners

Intermediate Outcomes

Increased use of evidence and policy analysis by Governors, JTB & SBIR

Agenda setting and prioritization of tax/revenue issues (via monthly briefings to Governors and quarterly meetings with the JTB)

Increased capacity of State governments to address domestic revenue challenges

Primary Outcome

Increased internally generated revenue and improved tax environment across the 36 States
